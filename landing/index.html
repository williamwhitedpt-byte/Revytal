How it works

Six steps. Zero added
workflow for the clinician.

From the moment a clinician types a note to the moment a supplier ships an order, Revytal handles the entire chain automatically.

01
Clinician enters note
The clinician types a note or dictates as usual. Revytal listens through the ambient layer. No new interface, no new step added to the workflow.
02
AI extracts clinical intent
Revytal parses clinical language and extracts structured product intent, anatomy, condition, product category, and specific items mentioned during the visit.
03
Curated SKU surfaced
Intent is matched against Revytal's clinical-grade product database. Clinic rules, supplier availability, and pricing are applied automatically no manual search.
04
Checkout link generated
A personalized checkout page is built for the patient. Each product tied to the visit, with plain-language context and a clean mobile experience ready to send.
05
Patient pays
Patient receives a link via SMS or QR code. They review their recommendations and complete a frictionless Stripe checkout. HSA-eligible items are clearly marked.
06
Order routed to supplier
Revytal dispatches the order directly to the correct supplier. Revenue is logged to the clinic dashboard in real time. The recommendation cycle is complete.
